About ARCMF ↑ Back to top

ARCOMM Mission Framework, or ARCMF for short, is the premiere mission framework for ARCOMM. Built on the great F3 Mission Framework by Folk ARPS, ARCMF has cut down significantly on unused and irrelevant components (and, by extension, file size) and instead has implemented more efficient and useful functions all around. As with the original F3, the guiding principle is still to enable potential mission makers to bring their idea to life without much hassle, yet also give them the ability to customise their mission to their hearts desire. This way, both new and seasoned mission makers can hop into ARCMF and start working immediately.

Currently, a new version of ARCMF, ARCMF2 is being developed by Kingsley and tryteyker. ARCMF2 will feature 3DEN Editor integration along with a shift of backend-assets to mod-form instead of being housed in the mission file. This change will allow ARCMF2's file size to be even smaller than its predecessor. Additionally, 3DEN Editor integration means that potential mission makers will no longer have to scour through dozens of SQF files to find what they need, and can instead customise their mission inside the editor itself, immensely improving workflow and providing more accessibility.
